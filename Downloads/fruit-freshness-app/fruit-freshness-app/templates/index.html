How this works

01

Pretrained eyes

A ResNet18 network, already trained on 1.2M photos, supplies the base visual understanding — edges, shapes, texture.

02

Fine-tuned on fruit

Its final layers were retrained on labeled photos of fresh and rotten produce until it learned what spoilage looks like.

03

Your photo, graded

Your upload is resized, normalized, and passed through the network, which returns a fruit type and a freshness call.

Built with PyTorch + torchvision + Flask.
